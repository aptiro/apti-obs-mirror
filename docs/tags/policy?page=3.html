Net Neutrality Guidelines in the Netherlands come into force on March 15, 2015
Wed 27 May 2015, 15:00

The Dutch Department of Economic Affairs issued guidelines on net neutrality for the Authority for Consumers and Markets (ACM). These Net Neutrality Guidelines serve as a basis for the enforcement by ACM of the net neutrality rules in Article 7.4a of the Dutch Telecommunications Act (Net Neutrality Act).

Dutch Scientific Council for Government Policy (WRR) published report on Internet
Tue 31 Mar 2015, 18:00

The report is sent to the Dutch Minister of Foreign Affairs, Bert Koenders regarding internet-related foreign policy. The report recommends the promotion of the norm that the core of the Internet - the central protocols and infrastructure that are a global public good - must be free of government interference; the promotion of different forms of security in relation to the Internet distinguished from each other nationally and internationally and are addressed by separate actors; and the expansion of the diplomatic field a part of the agenda for Internet diplomacy.

The English version of the report is available here. Please find a summary of the report here.
