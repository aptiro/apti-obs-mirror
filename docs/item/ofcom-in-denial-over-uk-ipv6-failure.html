Ofcom in denial over UK IPv6 failure

Experts say the UK has been more negative about the adoption of IPv6 than almost any other nation. The big ISPs convey a lack of urgency and Ofcom, rather than pushing industry to adopt, seems more focused on thinking of work-arounds. This article asks why the UK regulator fails to show leadership in IPv6 implementation.
